## üîó Overview of FI-SD Integration

FI-SD integration ensures that every sales transaction in SD automatically generates corresponding accounting entries in FI. This automation improves accuracy, reduces manual effort, and ensures real-time financial reporting.

---

## üß© Key Integration Points

### 1. **Sales Order Creation**
- No accounting document is generated.
- It‚Äôs a logistical step that sets the process in motion.

### 2. **Delivery and Post Goods Issue (PGI)**
- Inventory is reduced.
- Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) is posted.
- FI document is created:
  - **Debit:** COGS
  - **Credit:** Inventory

### 3. **Billing**
- Customer invoice is generated.
- Revenue and tax entries are posted in FI:
  - **Debit:** Customer Account (Receivable)
  - **Credit:** Revenue Account
  - **Credit:** Tax Account (if applicable)

---

## üõ†Ô∏è Configuration Steps

### üîß VKOA ‚Äì Account Determination
- VKOA is the transaction code used to configure account determination.
- It maps SD condition types (e.g., pricing, freight, discounts) to FI accounts using account keys.

### üîë Account Keys
Common account keys used in SD:
| Account Key | Purpose         |
|-------------|-----------------|
| ERL         | Revenue         |
| MWS         | Tax             |
| KOFI        | Freight         |
| VST         | Sales Tax       |

These keys are linked to G/L accounts in FI via VKOA.

### üìÅ Master Data Setup
- **Customer Master:** Must include reconciliation account.
- **Material Master:** Must include valuation class for inventory posting.

## üßæ Key Accounting Entries in FI-SD Integration

- **FI Accounting Entry:**

| Debit                  | Credit               |
|------------------------|----------------------|
| Customer Receivables   | Revenue Account      |
|                        | Tax Account (if applicable) |

- This entry reflects the income earned and the amount owed by the customer.
