Common SAP FICO Support Ticket Examples

Mock Ticket #1: Vendor Payment Failure in F110

Ticket ID: FICO-001

Module: Accounts Payable

Issue Summary: Vendor payment run executed via F110, but no accounting document was generated.

Root Cause: Payment method was not correctly assigned to the house bank in FBZP.

Resolution: Updated payment method configuration, reassigned the correct bank key, and re-executed the payment run.

Impact: Enabled successful posting of vendor payments and avoided delays in financial processing.

Complicated SAP FICO Support Ticket Scenarios

1. Intercompany Reconciliation Failure

Issue: Intercompany transactions between two company codes were not reconciling due to missing configuration in the IC clearing accounts.

Resolution: Analyzed document splitting, checked intercompany settings in OBYA, and corrected GL account assignments. Coordinated with both company code owners to reprocess transactions and ensure proper elimination in consolidation.

2. Incorrect Exchange Rate Impacting Foreign Currency Valuation

Issue: Month-end valuation (FAGL_FCV) posted incorrect unrealized gains/losses due to outdated exchange rates.

Resolution: Investigated OB08 entries, identified missing rate types for specific currencies, and updated rates. Reversed incorrect valuation postings and re-executed the run with correct data.

3. Asset Transfer Not Posting Correctly Across Company Codes

Issue: Asset transfer between company codes failed with error ‚ÄúTransaction type not allowed for cross-company code transfer.‚Äù

Resolution: Reviewed transaction types in AO73, ensured correct configuration in intercompany asset transfer settings, and validated asset master data. Coordinated with asset accounting and controlling teams to complete the transfer.

4. Profit Center Not Deriving in Vendor Invoice

Issue: Vendor invoice posted without profit center, causing issues in reporting and CO reconciliation.

Resolution: Traced derivation logic using OKB9 and substitution rules in GGB1. Identified missing cost object assignment and updated master data to ensure automatic profit center derivation.

5. Payment Medium File Not Generating in F110

Issue: Payment run completed but no payment medium file was generated for bank transmission.

Resolution: Checked DMEE tree configuration, payment method settings in FBZP, and house bank setup. Corrected format tree assignment and regenerated the file successfully.

6. Document Splitting Inconsistency

Issue: Document splitting failed during posting, causing imbalance in financial statements.

Resolution: Reviewed splitting rules in SPRO, analyzed item categories and business transactions. Adjusted configuration and reprocessed the document with correct segment assignment.

7. Tax Reporting Discrepancy in GST/VAT

Issue: GST reports showed mismatched tax amounts due to incorrect tax code usage across transactions.

Resolution: Audited tax procedure, validated FTXP and OBYZ settings, and corrected master data. Provided training to users and implemented validation rules to prevent future errors.
